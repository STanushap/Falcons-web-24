Our departments

What We do?

The premier Advanced Class Aerodesign team at VIT University, renowned for our rich legacy and expertise in crafting intricate RC aircrafts, aimed at addressing real-world challenges.

Design

Design is a crucial aspect of aircraft construction. We prioritize in-depth design research to create aircraft that earn admiration from industry giants like... Design is a crucial aspect of aircraft construction. We prioritize in-depth design research to create aircraft that earn admiration from industry giants like Lockheed Martin and Boeing. Our process considers various factors like control surface sizing, drag reduction, crosswind handling, and airfoil and fuselage analysis. Each aircraft is meticulously crafted for optimal performance in specific conditions. Following extensive research, our design team refines factors such as wing dimensions for lift, tail dimensions for stability, and control surface design for roll rate and crosswind handling.

Structures

This department utilizes SolidWorks for 3D modeling to visually represent aircraft designs based on specified parameters, aiming to... This department utilizes SolidWorks for 3D modeling to visually represent aircraft designs based on specified parameters, aiming to make them more realistic. They conduct structural analysis using software such as ANSYS Static Structural module. Their main focus is on creating robust yet lightweight structures through the use of extensively fabricated composites in-house. Additionally, they are responsible for designing efficient and dependable payload dropping mechanisms for carrying and releasing maximum payloads, as well as the aircraft's landing gear system.

Avionics

The avionics department designs and integrates electronic systems that match the aircraft's standards for control and communication. They... The avionics department designs and integrates electronic systems that match the aircraft's standards for control and communication. They utilize both commercially available flight controllers and microcontrollers to develop and test their logics, incorporating various sensors and actuators. Their goal extends beyond competition mission requirements, delving into UAVs and autonomous flight systems. This involves creating custom algorithms, efficient electronics, and employing computer vision and image processing techniques.

CFD

Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) is conducted using ANSYS Fluent and Star CCM+ to verify the outcomes provided by the design department... Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) is conducted using ANSYS Fluent and Star CCM+ to verify the outcomes provided by the design department. This process provides precise estimations of Lift, Drag, and Pressure Distribution linked with the aircraft. These results enable us to identify areas of maximum drag or minimum lift, facilitating adjustments to enhance the overall aerodynamic profile of the aircraft. The simulation depicts airflow distribution around the aircraft design under conditions of low subsonic, incompressible flow.

Management

Management handles the daily operations of the team, focusing on maintaining an efficient work environment and facilitating a swift workflow. They juggle numerous tasks, including event coordination, personnel management, and overseeing departmental activities. Additionally, they manage sponsorships and documentation, which are crucial for the team's credibility and financial backing. Managing the team's social media presence is also part of their responsibilities, aiding in establishing a strong global presence.

Propulsion

The propulsion system is essential for an aircraft, providing the necessary power to propel it forward. Our aircraft's propulsion system incorporates either an electric motor or an IC engine, which drives a propeller to generate thrust. Without a sufficiently powerful propulsion system, a lightweight aircraft with high lift capacity would be ineffective. At Assailing Falcons, we conduct experiments with various propellers to determine the best fit for our competition missions. This propeller analysis, combined with our efficient aircraft design, has earned us numerous international awards.

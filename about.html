Our team

Assailing Falcons a highly esteemed member of SAE International, is a student organized team of diverse and talented individuals with a shared passion for aerodesign. Our team's primary objective is to foster the growth and advancement of students, equipping them with rare and valuable practical knowledge and expert guidance. Through our endeavors, we aim to facilitate the students' educational journey, enhance their technical expertise, foster accomplishment, and refine their skill sets.

Our Mission

As a team, we aim to grab the highest laurels for the country and construct aerodynamically efficient aircraft. As a team we not only reach the goal of our mission statement, but also intricate ourselves with the depths of knowledge and research for further field development. We passionately work to create airplanes that surpass competition standards, aiming for capabilities beyond the norm.
